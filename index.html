Who We Are

Building the Future of Automation

V Automation System is a New Delhi-based technology company specializing in IT solutions and Building Management System (BMS) automation. We design, implement, and maintain smart infrastructure systems that optimize energy efficiency, security, and operational control for commercial and residential environments.

From HVAC control systems to integrated fire safety networks and structured cabling, we bring expertise across a wide spectrum of building technologies. Our team is committed to delivering solutions that are not only technically robust, but also intuitive and easy to manage.
